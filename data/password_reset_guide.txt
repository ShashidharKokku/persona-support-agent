PASSWORD RESET & ACCOUNT ACCESS GUIDE
=======================================

HOW TO RESET YOUR PASSWORD
---------------------------
Step 1: Go to our login page at app.ourplatform.com/login
Step 2: Click the "Forgot Password?" link below the login form
Step 3: Enter your registered email address and click "Send Reset Link"
Step 4: Check your inbox for an email from noreply@ourplatform.com
Step 5: Click the reset link inside the email (valid for 15 minutes only)
Step 6: Enter your new password and confirm it
Step 7: You will be automatically logged in after a successful reset

PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS
----------------------
Your new password must meet these security requirements:
- Minimum 8 characters in length
- At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- At least one number (0-9)
- At least one special character (!, @, #, $, %, etc.)
- Cannot be the same as your last 5 passwords

IF YOU DON'T RECEIVE THE RESET EMAIL
--------------------------------------
- Check your spam or junk mail folder first
- Ensure you are using the exact email address registered to your account
- Wait up to 5 minutes — email delivery can sometimes be delayed
- Add noreply@ourplatform.com to your safe senders list
- Try requesting another reset link if 10 minutes have passed
- Contact support@ourplatform.com if the problem continues

ACCOUNT LOCKED OUT
-------------------
Your account gets temporarily locked after 5 consecutive failed login attempts.

- Lockout duration: 30 minutes automatic unlock
- To unlock immediately: use the "Forgot Password?" flow to reset your credentials
- If your account is locked due to suspicious activity, contact security@ourplatform.com

TWO-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION (2FA)
---------------------------------
If you have 2FA enabled and lost access to your authenticator app:
1. Click "Use a backup code" on the 2FA screen
2. Enter one of the 8-digit backup codes saved during setup
3. If backup codes are also lost, contact support with a government-issued ID for identity verification

CHANGING YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS
-----------------------------
To change the email address on your account:
1. Log in and go to Settings > Account > Email
2. Enter your new email address and current password
3. A verification link will be sent to your NEW email address
4. Click the link to confirm the change
5. You will be logged out and must log in with the new email

COOKIES AND BROWSER ISSUES
----------------------------
If you cannot log in even with the correct password, try these steps:
1. Clear your browser cookies and cache (Settings > Privacy > Clear browsing data)
2. Try an incognito/private browsing window
3. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
4. Disable browser extensions temporarily
5. Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser settings

SESSION MANAGEMENT
-------------------
- Sessions expire automatically after 30 minutes of inactivity
- You can manage active sessions from Settings > Security > Active Sessions
- Click "Revoke" next to any session to remotely log out that device
- Logging in from an unrecognized device will trigger an email notification
