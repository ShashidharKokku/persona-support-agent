BILLING POLICY & SUPPORT GUIDE
================================

SUBSCRIPTION PLANS
------------------
Basic Plan:     $9/month  — 10,000 API calls, email support, 99.5% uptime SLA
Pro Plan:       $49/month — 100,000 API calls, priority support, 99.9% uptime SLA
Enterprise:     Custom pricing — Unlimited calls, dedicated support, 99.99% uptime SLA

All plans are billed monthly. Annual billing available at 20% discount.

REFUND POLICY
-------------
- Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method
- Duplicate charges are identified automatically and refunded within 2 business days
- Partial refunds are available for unused days if you downgrade mid-cycle
- No refunds are issued for accounts suspended due to Terms of Service violations

HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND
------------------------
1. Log in to your account dashboard
2. Navigate to Billing > Transaction History
3. Click "Dispute" next to the relevant charge
4. Fill in the reason and submit
5. You will receive a confirmation email within 24 hours
6. Alternatively, email billing@support.com with your invoice number

BILLING DISPUTES
----------------
To dispute a charge:
- Contact billing@support.com within 60 days of the charge
- Include your account email, invoice number, and a brief description
- Our billing team will investigate and respond within 3 business days
- If a chargeback is filed with your bank, account access may be temporarily suspended

PAYMENT METHODS
---------------
Accepted: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Bank Transfer (Enterprise only)
All transactions are encrypted using PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant systems.

INVOICE & RECEIPTS
------------------
- Invoices are automatically emailed on your billing date each month
- Download past invoices from Billing > Invoice History in your dashboard
- VAT invoices available for EU customers — update your VAT number in account settings

PLAN UPGRADES & DOWNGRADES
---------------------------
- Upgrades take effect immediately; you are charged a prorated amount
- Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle
- Cancellations: Cancel anytime; access continues until the end of the paid period

OPERATIONAL UPTIME & SLA
--------------------------
- We target 99.9% uptime on Pro and above plans
- Scheduled maintenance is communicated 48 hours in advance via email
- SLA credits are issued automatically if uptime falls below the guarantee
- Credit amount: 10% of monthly fee per hour of excess downtime, capped at 30%

ENTERPRISE BILLING
------------------
Enterprise customers receive:
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom invoicing and NET-30/NET-60 payment terms
- Volume discounts starting at 100,000 API calls/month
- Contact sales@ourplatform.com for custom quotes and contracts
